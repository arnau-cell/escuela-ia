Answer five questions and we'll give you a concrete route: what to install, why, how much it costs, and what alternatives you have. No AI involved — it's a deterministic rules engine, so the same answers always give you the same route, and you can see exactly why it's recommended.

## If you want to change routes later

None of this is final. If your goal changes, you get a better GPU, or your budget changes, just fill out the form again — the recommendation recalculates instantly, with nothing about your answers saved on any server.
