## What it is

Easy AI (provisional name; the final one will be set before launching with its own
domain) is a free, neutral, bilingual (Spanish/English) site to understand artificial intelligence from
zero to a technical level: what each thing is, what it's for, when to use one tool over another, what it
costs, and how to set up your own AI setup based on your computer and budget.

## Mission

That anyone — regardless of their starting level — can genuinely understand AI, not just use it blindly.
The [Learn](/en/learn/start-here/) section goes from zero knowledge to technical level by levels;
the [Easy AI core](/en/) turns that into a concrete plan for your case;
[By industry](/en/by-industry/) connects the theory to real cases in your profession.

It's not a business — it's a project meant to help, at minimal cost. Monetization is not the focus.

## Neutrality policy

- **No paid affiliates.** No tool mentioned on this site pays to be featured or recommended.
- **Always open alternatives.** When a paid or closed tool is mentioned, a free or open-source
  alternative is looked up and listed alongside it, when a comparable one exists.
- **Costs with a verification date.** Prices and features change; tables with volatile data carry a
  `lastVerified` field with the date it was last checked against the official source (not a
  third-party aggregator).
- **No advertising or tracking.** The site's analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) are aggregate and
  cookieless — see the [cookie policy](/en/resources/cookies/) for the verified detail, not just a claim.

## How it's built

The repository has been public from day one:
[github.com/arnau-cell/escuela-ia](https://github.com/arnau-cell/escuela-ia). Every block of work is
built and audited in separate sessions before being considered done — the blog entry
[how we built the news pipeline](/en/share/how-i-built-it/news-pipeline-n8n/) tells a real example of
that process, bugs included, not just the polished result.

Is something explained wrong, out of date, or missing? The project accepts contributions via Pull
Request — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/arnau-cell/escuela-ia/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md)
or open an [Issue](https://github.com/arnau-cell/escuela-ia/issues).
